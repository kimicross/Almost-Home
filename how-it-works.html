  • Bundle pricing takes effect on the second item purchased

  • I'm not responsible for lost, stolen or damaged packages. All items shipped include tracking through Paypal. The best way to prevent a loss is to purchase additional insurance for a purchase worth over $50. If you would like to include additional insurance, please mention this when claiming your item.
